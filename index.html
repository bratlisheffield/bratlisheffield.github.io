About the Artist

Hello! My name is Sunniva, and I'm a ceramic artist based in Oslo. I find inspiration in the natural world, which often leads to earthy tones, organic textures, and functional forms. Each piece is hand-thrown and individually glazed in my home studio. My goal is to create objects that bring a moment of peace and beauty to everyday life.

*Specializing in functional dinnerware and sculptural forms.*
